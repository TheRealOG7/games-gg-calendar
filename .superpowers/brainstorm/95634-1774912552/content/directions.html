Which direction feels right?

Pick one or more that match your vision for the calendar. These aren't mutually exclusive.

A

Wishlist / Hype Tracker

Users click a ❤️ or 🔥 on any game to add it to their personal watchlist. Stored locally (no login). A sidebar or panel shows "Your upcoming games" sorted by release date — a personalized view of the calendar.

B

Hype Countdowns

When you open a game's popover, a live countdown ticks down to release — "23 days, 4 hrs". On release day, the cell gets a special glow/shimmer animation and a "Out Now 🎮" badge. Visual celebration of the moment.

C

Community Hype Votes

Each game shows a hype score — users upvote what they're excited about. A "Most Hyped" section surfaces the top games this month. No login needed (IP or fingerprint based). Adds a social pulse to the calendar.

D

Visual Delight & Micro-animations

No accounts, just polish: confetti burst when a big release drops, animated number counters on stats, smooth cell hover reveals, particle effect on "today" cell, ambient glow on events by category colour. Makes it feel alive.

E

Visit Streaks & Badges

Track how many days in a row the user visits (localStorage). Show a small flame streak counter in the header. Unlock cosmetic badges: "Week Watcher 🔥", "Release Hunter 🎯", "Esports Fan ⚔️". Subtle but sticky.
