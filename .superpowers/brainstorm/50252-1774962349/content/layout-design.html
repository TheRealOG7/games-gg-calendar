Panel 1 — Calendar
Existing monthly grid. Cells show colored dots instead of pills (more compact). Clicking a date opens the day panel. Selected date highlighted in green.
Panel 2 — Day View
Game art in 3-col portrait grid. Events (esports, conventions) shown as full-width cards below. Watchlist + button on each cover. Slides in on date click.
Panel 3 — Detail
Appears when you click a game card. Cover art, title, metacritic, genres, platforms, watchlist button, add to calendar. Slides in from the right.
